Recurring cleans

Get your weekends back.

Most people who hire a cleaner do it once and stop — because the rotation of strangers in their home is exhausting in its own way. We solve that by assigning one dedicated cleaner to your home, every visit.

Over time, they learn the place. They know which surfaces need extra attention, where you keep things, what your standards look like. The clean gets better, not worse.

  • All living areas, bedrooms & bathrooms
  • Kitchen — including stovetop, splashback, microwave
  • Vacuum, mop, dust, polish
  • Bins emptied, beds made, tidy left behind
  • Eco-friendly products available on request
  • 10–15% off compared to one-off rates

The deep clean, when you need it.

Sometimes you don't need a regular cleaner — you just need the place reset. Spring cleans, post-party recovery, pre-inspection sparkles, getting the house ready for guests.
