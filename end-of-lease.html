End-of-Lease FAQ

Common questions.

How long does an end-of-lease clean take?

A 1-bedroom apartment usually takes 3–4 hours with a 2-person crew. A 3-bedroom house typically takes 5–7 hours. We send the right crew size to finish in one day.

Is carpet steam cleaning included?

Carpet steam cleaning is an optional add-on (most leases require it). We can arrange it as part of your booking — pricing depends on the number of rooms and stair runs.

What if my agent isn't happy with something?

Notify us within 72 hours of the clean and we'll return to re-clean any flagged areas at no extra cost. We've never had a tenant lose bond when our checklist was followed.

Do you clean external windows or balconies?

Internal windows are included as standard. External windows (up to 2 storeys) and balconies can be added on request — they're often required for ground-floor and apartment leases.

Can I book you the day before my final inspection?

Yes — but earlier is better. We recommend booking 1–2 weeks ahead during peak season (December–February). For last-minute bookings, give us a call and we'll do everything we can.
