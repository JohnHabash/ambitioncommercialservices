Our story

A cleaning service that actually answers the phone.

Ambition started in 2026 with a small crew, a strong opinion about how cleaning services should be run, and a CRM. We don't outsource. We don't subcontract. Every cleaner who shows up wears our uniform, knows our standards, and reports to us.

That structural simplicity lets us promise things bigger operators can't: the same crew every visit, two-hour quote turnaround, direct contact with someone who can actually make decisions.

We're growing carefully. Quality first, scale second.

Our values

Three principles. No exceptions.

Consistency over volume. We'd rather have 50 happy recurring clients than 500 one-off jobs. Recurring relationships keep our crews steady and our standards high.

Direct communication. No call centres, no ticket queues. Every client has the direct number of someone who can solve problems on the spot.

Pay cleaners well. Better pay attracts better people. Better people deliver better cleans. The economics are obvious — most operators just don't bother.
