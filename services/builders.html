Builders clean.
Stage 1 — Builders Clean

Rough-in cleaning, before the trades come back.

Once the major construction work is done but before painters, electricians and finishers return, sites need a thorough rough clean. Removing the dust, gyprock waste and debris that gets in the way of finishing trades — and damages new finishes if left.

  • Removal of construction debris & waste
  • Dust removal from all surfaces, including high-level
  • Vacuum and sweep all rooms
  • Sticker, label & protective film removal
  • Initial wipe of fixtures, fittings & joinery
  • Skip and disposal coordination if required

Sparkle clean.
Stage 2 — Final Sparkle Clean

Handover-ready. Detail-obsessed.

The final sparkle clean is what separates a good handover from a great one. Every surface detailed, every fingerprint removed, every fixture polished — ready for client walk-through, photography, or first occupancy.

  • Full detail clean of every room
  • Inside cabinets, drawers & built-ins
  • Windows — internal & external (up to 2 storeys)
  • Polish all stainless steel, glass & chrome
  • Tile and grout detailing
  • Light fittings, switches, power points
  • Floor finishing — hard floors mopped, carpets vacuumed
  • Final dust pass — all horizontal surfaces
